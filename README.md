# Excel Data Visualisation and Dashboarding Project

## Project Summary
*This Excel project analyses 2,156 sales and shipping transactions from 2019 to 2020. The dataset includes customers, products, order dates, salespeople, regions, countries, quantities, discounts, sales values, and freight information.*

*I used the data to build an interactive dashboard and summary report that make sales performance easier to explore, compare, and understand.*
<br><br>

## Business Questions
*The project analyses sales performance across time, salespeople, regions, and countries. It is designed to answer four key business questions:*

1. How are sales and order activity changing over time?
2. Which salespeople and regions contribute most to total sales?
3. How does the selected year's performance compare with the previous year?
4. Which countries have the strongest sales concentration?
<br><br>

## Outcome
*The completed report produced the following key findings:*  

* 2020 generated **$776,243 from 493 orders**, compared with **$489,550 from 337 orders** in 2019.
* **Q3 2020** was the strongest quarter, generating **$305,196**.
* **October** was the highest-performing month, generating **$129,247 from 77 orders**.
* **Sam Achebe** was the top salesperson, generating **$127,117**, or **16.4%** of 2020 sales. Gio Maletti contributed 15.7%, followed by Chloe Summers at 15.1%.
* **Europe** was the largest sales region, generating **$462,403**, or approximately **59.6%** of 2020 sales.
* All nine salespeople improved their sales compared with 2019. **Lisa Beddard** recorded the highest growth at **267%**, followed by Maya Diaz at 111% and Sam Achebe at 96%.
* Sales were concentrated in several key countries: **USA, Germany, and Austria** generated a combined **$386,851**, accounting for **49.8%** of 2020 sales.
<br>

*By combining the dashboard and summary report, I created a tool that allows users to filter results, compare yearly performance, and identify sales patterns without reviewing more than 2,000 individual records.*

*Sales managers, regional managers, and business analysts can use the report to recognise top performers, investigate weaker periods or markets, allocate resources more effectively, and focus future sales efforts on the strongest opportunities.*
